Bio

I got my Ph.D. degree at University at Buffalo under the advisory of Prof. Jing Gao. I got my B.Eng. degree from the Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications.

Research

My research lies in the intersection of data mining/machine learning and security. I mainly focus on analyzing the vulnerability of various data mining/machine learning models (e.g., knowledge graphs embedding, recommendation models) and developing defense methods to improve the robustness of these models. I am also passinate about applying the adversarial learning techniques to solve problems in NLP applications.
